Who We Are?

"Out Focus is Youth Empowerment"

Youth Assembly is launched to engage youth in in healthy discourse and expose them to the democratic process and practices. Members of Youth Assembly are selected for the duration a year at a time. Establishment and facilitation of the Youth Assembly KP is a focus on youth in the country and youth’s awareness education and training in the norms of politics and democracy in the country.
