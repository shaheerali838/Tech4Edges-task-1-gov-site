About Ittehad Party

Ittehad Party is built on the foundation of unity and collaboration. We believe that through collective efforts and mutual understanding, we can achieve remarkable progress for all communities in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

Our party focuses on inter-community harmony, inclusive development, and cooperative governance. We work to bridge divides and create platforms where diverse voices can come together to address common challenges and pursue shared goals.

With a commitment to peaceful coexistence and collaborative problem-solving, Ittehad Party members are actively involved in initiatives that promote social cohesion, cultural exchange, and unified community development across the province.
