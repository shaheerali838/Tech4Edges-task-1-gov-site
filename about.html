Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Youth Assembly

"Our Focus is Youth Empowerment"

The Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Youth Assembly (KPYA) offers numerous benefits that positively impact the development and future of young individuals. Some of the key advantages include:

  • Enhanced Leadership Skills: The KPYA provides young people with opportunities to develop leadership and decision-making skills, offering practical training in policy-making and governance.
  • Educational and Social Awareness: Through workshops, seminars, and debates, participants gain a deeper understanding of educational and social issues, engaging in discussions and analysis of national and international matters.
  • Practical Training in Politics and Democracy: The assembly offers a democratic platform where young individuals can gain hands-on experience in parliamentary procedures, learning about legislation, policy design, and governmental structures.
  • Communication and Networking Opportunities: Participants connect with like-minded peers, academics, policymakers, and government representatives, fostering networks that can benefit their future careers and social development.
  • Role in Addressing Social Issues: Members discuss social issues and propose solutions, presenting their ideas to authorities and contributing to implementation efforts.
  • Engaging Youth in Positive Activities: The KPYA keeps young people engaged in constructive activities, steering them away from negative influences and involving them in welfare projects and community development initiatives.
  • Career Development Opportunities: The assembly provides training programs and internship opportunities, allowing youth to showcase their skills nationally and internationally.

Overall, the KPYA serves as an excellent platform for young individuals interested in politics, social services, and policy-making, empowering them to contribute positively to their province and country.

YAP have 4 Provincial Wings:

  • KP Youth Assembly
  • Punjab Youth Assembly
  • Sindh Youth Assembly
  • Balochistan Youth Assembly
